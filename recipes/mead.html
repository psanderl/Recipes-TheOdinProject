Traditional Mead

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs honey
  • 1 gallon water
  • 1 packet yeast
  • 1/2 cup raisins(optional)
  • 1/2 orange or lemon zest

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot heat the honey and water over medium heat until honey is dissolved. Do Not Boil
  2. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature,then transfer to a sterilized fermenting container.
  3. Add the yeast and flavorings (raisins and citrus zest). Seal the fermenting container with an airlock .
  4. Allow the mead to ferment for 2-4 weeks, or until the bubbling in the airlock slows down.
  5. You can drink it now, however it will be cloudy and unaged. To clarify and age continue to next step.
  6. Rack the mead into a secondary fermenting container,leaving sediment behind. Allow the mead to age for an additional 2-6 months.
  7. Bottle and enjoy from a Viking Horn. (Or a glass, I'm not picky.)
