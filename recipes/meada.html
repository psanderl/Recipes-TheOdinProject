Traditional Mead Non-Alcoholic Alternative

Ingredients:

  • 1 litre water
  • 340 grams honey
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground giner
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 orange

Instructions:

  1. Bring the water, honey, nutmeg, ginger and cinnamon to a boil in a non-metallic pan.
  2. Slice the orange and lemon.
  3. Stir until honey is dissolved. Use a wooden spoon to skim off any skin that forms at the top of the brew.
  4. Add lemon and orange slices, squeezing as they are placed in the pan. Cool completely.
  5. Strain & refrigerate.
  6. Enjoy from a Viking Horn. (Or a glass, I'm not picky.)
