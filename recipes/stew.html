The Odin Project-Stew

Viking Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef or venison, cubed
  • 2 onions,chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 2 parsnips, chopped
  • 2 turnips, chopped
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried sage
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
